Are tropical rainforests a carbon source or a carbon sink?

Yujie Wang, 2017-11-29

There was a recent research published on "Science" about the carbon cycle in tropical ranforest ( DOI). The research argued that the tropical rainforests were a carbon source from aboveground observations from 2003 to 2014.
